## Value Proposition: "Why Join NS Marketplace"

### For Service Providers
- **Zero friction listing** - Post your service in < 2 minutes
- **Native NS community reach** - Everyone at KL/NS sees your listing
- **Discord-native contact** - Use the communication tool you already use
- **Free to start** - No upfront costs or platform fees (for now)
- **Simple, Craigslist-style** - No complex setup, just post and go

### For Product Sellers
- **Local NS market** - Sell directly to your neighbors
- **Skip the middleman** - No shipping, no platform fees
- **Fast deals** - Meet up, transact, done
- **Community trust** - Trading within a known community

## Onboarding Process

### Phase 1: Initial Contact (30 min)
1. Create definitive list of NS businesses
2. Gather Discord handles/contact info
3. Draft personalized DM/message template

### Phase 2: Schedule Calls (30 min)
Book 15-20 min 1-on-1 with each business owner

### Phase 3: Conduct Onboarding (3-4 hrs total)
Per business:
- Walk through the platform
- Show them how to post listings
- Answer questions
- Get commitment to post at least 1 listing

### Phase 4: Create Profiles (2 hrs)
Help them:
- Take/upload photos
- Write compelling descriptions
- Set competitive pricing
- Add their Discord contact

### Phase 5: Launch & Promote (30 min)
- Announce newly listed businesses in NS channels
- Share specific listings
- Drive initial traffic to marketplace

### Phase 6: Follow-up (1 hr after 2 weeks)
- Check metrics (views, contacts, sales)
- Gather feedback
- Iterate on platform features
- Adjust onboarding approach

## Success Metrics

Track per business:
- Number of listings posted
- Views per listing
- Contact rate (how many people clicked "Contact")
- Completed transactions (self-reported)
- Satisfaction score (1-10)
- Would they recommend to other NS businesses?
